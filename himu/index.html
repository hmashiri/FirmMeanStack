About Attorney Alex Mashiri

The Founder and Principal of the Firm, Alex Asil Mashiri, has received a bachelorâ€™s degree in Political Science and Public Law from the University of California San Diego and a Juris Doctor degree from Thomas Jefferson School of Law where he graduated Magna Cum Laude. During law school, he received several awards including the Witkin Award for Academic Excellence, Jefferson Medal and Cali Excellence for the Future Award in Appellate Advocacy, Business Associations, Wills and Trusts and Legal Synthesis. In addition, Attorney Mashiri developed strong legal research and writing skills by externing with Associate Justice Judith L. Haller at the California Court of Appeal, Fourth District, Division One and with Judges Robert J. Kearney and Runston Maino at the San Diego Superior Court. While at the San Diego Superior Court, Attorney Mashiri had the privilege of researching, drafting and updating the Duty Judge Manual, which is currently being used by every trial judge in San Diego County. Before becoming an Attorney, he worked both as a paralegal and office manager at a prestigious Civil Litigation Firm for several years. With his combined knowledge and experience, Attorney Mashiri works with his clients to achieve the best possible result in each case. Attorney Mashiri treats his clients as family. He is always attentive to his clients and available to answer any questions.
